Do you support the right to repair? Support the Digital Fair Repair Act

The New York State Assembly is currently debating the Digital Fair Repair Act, a bill which would

require original equipment manufacturers to make diagnostic and repair information for digital electronic parts and equipment available to independent repair providers and consumers if such parts and repair information are also available to OEM authorized repair providers.

If you suport this bill, we encourage you to reach out to your state assembly member (you can find them here!), and also sign our petition below!
